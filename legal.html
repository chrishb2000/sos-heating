Privacy Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

At SOS Plumbing & Electric Services, we are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you visit our website or use our services.

Cookie Policy

Last Updated: January 2024

This Cookie Policy explains how SOS Plumbing & Electric Services uses cookies and similar technologies on our website. It explains what cookies are, how we use them, and how you can control them.

1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device (computer, smartphone, or tablet) when you visit a website. They help websites remember information about your visit, which can make it easier to visit the site again and make the site more useful to you.

2. Types of Cookies We Use

Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable basic functions like page navigation and access to secure areas of the website. Without these cookies, the website cannot function properly.

Performance Cookies

These cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website by collecting and reporting information anonymously. This helps us improve the way our website works.

Functional Cookies

These cookies allow the website to remember choices you make (such as your language preference or the region you are in) and provide enhanced, more personalized features.

3. Specific Cookies We Use

  • Language Preference Cookie: Remembers your selected language preference for the website.
  • Cookie Consent Cookie: Remembers whether you have accepted our cookie policy.
  • Analytics Cookies: Help us understand website traffic and visitor behavior.

4. How to Control Cookies

You have the right to decide whether to accept or reject cookies. You can exercise your cookie preferences through your browser settings:

Browser Settings

Most web browsers allow you to control cookies through their settings. You can set your browser to accept all cookies, reject all cookies, notify you when a cookie is set, or delete cookies periodically.
